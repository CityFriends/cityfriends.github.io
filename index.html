ProposalOps

A cloud-native, Shipley-aligned proposal development tool

📌 Overview

This prototype outlines a modular proposal development workflow based on the Shipley Business Development Lifecycle. Each section is designed to guide teams through planning, writing, reviewing, and submitting successful government proposals.

1. 🧭 Capture Planning

  • Opportunity Summary
  • Teaming Partners
  • SWOT Analysis
  • Win Themes

2. 🎯 Bid/No-Bid Decision Framework

  • Strategic Fit Assessment
  • Capability Alignment
  • Resource Readiness
  • Go/No-Go Scorecard

3. 🛠 Proposal Planning

  • Proposal Schedule & Milestones
  • Team Roles & Responsibilities
  • Kickoff Briefing Template
  • Writer’s Guidelines

4. ✍️ Content Development

  • Section-by-Section Templates
  • Boilerplate Content Library
  • AI-Powered Outline Generator (coming soon)
  • Upload/Link Drafts

5. 📊 Compliance Matrix

  • Import from RFP
  • Auto-generated Matrix
  • Track Compliance & Status
  • Export to CSV/PDF

6. 🧪 Color Team Reviews

  • Pink Team (Outline/Concept)
  • Red Team (First Full Draft)
  • Gold Team (Pre-submission Polish)
  • Reviewer Feedback Logs

7. ✅ Submission

  • Submission Checklist
  • Cover Letter Template
  • PDF Merge Tool
  • Confirmation Uploads

8. 📚 Post-Submission Feedback

  • Debrief Tracker
  • Lessons Learned Log
  • Proposal Retrospective Template

🔗 Coming Soon

  • Claude-powered outline + win theme generator
  • Interactive forms for bid/no-bid and compliance tracking
  • User login, reviewer dashboard
